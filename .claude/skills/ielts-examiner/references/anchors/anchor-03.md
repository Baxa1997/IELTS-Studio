# Anchor 03 — Band 5.0

Original, expert-verification pending. Use to anchor weak-but-attempting scripts —
ideas are relevant but development and language control are limited.

## Prompt

Some people believe that children should begin formal schooling at a very early
age, while others think they should start later. Discuss both views and give your
own opinion.

## Essay (≈ 250 words)

Nowadays, many parent want to know what is the best age for their children to
start the school. Some people think children must go to school very early, but
other people believe is better to wait. In this essay i will discuss both opinion
and give my view.

On the one hand, some peoples think early education is good. When children go to
school in early age, they can learn many thing like reading and writing faster
than other children. Also they can make friends and they not feel shy when they
grow up. This is the reason why some parent send their child to school when they
are only three or four years old.

On the other hand, other people think this is not a good idea. They say small
children need to play and stay with they family, because childhood is very
important time. If a child study too much in early age, maybe he will be tired and
stress, and he can lose his happiness. So they prefer the children start school
when they are six or seven.

In my opinion, i think both views have some true. But i believe is better the
children start a little later, because playing is also a way of learning. In
conclusion, the age of starting school is depend on the child, but parents should
not force them too early.

## Calibrated bands — overall **5.0**

- **TR 5** — Both views are mentioned and a position is given, but the ideas are
  limited and only partly developed: support is brief and repetitive ("learn
  faster", "make friends") with no real extension (TR-UNDERDEV).
- **CC 5** — There are paragraphs and a basic frame ("On the one hand… On the
  other hand… In my opinion… In conclusion"), but progression inside the body is
  weak, the linking is mechanical, and referencing slips ("stay with they family")
  force the reader to re-read (CC-MECHANICAL, CC-REFERENCING).
- **LR 5** — Limited, repetitive vocabulary with noticeable errors that cause some
  difficulty: word-form ("tired and stress" for *stressed*), word choice ("have
  some true" for *truth*), and frequent imprecision (LR-BASIC, LR-FORMATION).
- **GRA 5** — A limited range with frequent errors: subject–verb agreement ("many
  parent", "peoples", "a child study"), missing verbs/articles ("is better the
  children start"), and "is depend". Complex attempts are markedly less accurate
  than the simple sentences (GRA-AGREEMENT, GRA-ARTICLE, GRA-DENSITY).

## Score-blocker

**GRA / LR (jointly)** — the ideas are relevant, but frequent basic errors and a
narrow, repetitive vocabulary are what hold every criterion at 5.

## Band with fixes → ~6.0

Fix subject–verb agreement and articles, and stop repeating the same few words;
then extend each view with one explained example. The structure is already close
to Band 6 — language control is the ceiling.
