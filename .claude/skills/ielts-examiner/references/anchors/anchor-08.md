## Prompt

In many countries the number of people choosing to live alone is increasing. What
are the reasons for this, and is it a positive or negative development?

## Essay (≈ 280 words)

In recent years, more and more people are deciding to live alone instead of living
with their family or a partner. There are several reasons for this trend, and in my
opinion it is a development that has both positive and negative sides.

There are two main reasons why people choose to live alone. Firstly, many young
people today have a good job and enough money, so they can afford to rent or buy
their own house. They want to have freedom and privacy, which is difficult when you
live with other people. Secondly, the society has changed and now it is normal to
get married later or not get married at all. Because of this, a lot of people stay
single for a longer time and they live by themself.

In my opinion, this trend has some advantages. People who live alone can be more
independent and they learn how to take care of everything by their own, such as
cooking and paying the bills. However, there are also some disadvantages. Living
alone can make people feel lonely, especially old people who don't have anyone to
talk with. It can also be more expensive because one person must pay all the cost.

In conclusion, the main reasons for living alone are financial independence and the
change in the society. Although it helps people to become more independent, I
believe it can also cause loneliness and other problems, so it is both a positive
and negative development depend on the situation.
