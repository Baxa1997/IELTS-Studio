## Prompt

Some people think it is better to live in a big city, while others prefer the
countryside. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

## Essay (≈ 200 words)

I want to talk about live in big city or countryside. This is very difficult
question for everybody and many people have different idea about this topic.

Big city is good because many thing to do. You can find job easy and many shop and
restaurant. The transport also good you can go everywhere by bus or train. But big
city have too much people and the air is not clean, sometime i feel the city is very
noise and crowded so it make me tired.

Countryside is very quiet and the air is fresh and clean. The people in countryside
is very kind and friendly. But there is no much job and the young people they go to
city for find work. Also countryside have no big hospital and good school for the
children.

So both place have good and bad thing. For me i think it depend on the person what
they like and what is their situation in the life. Everybody must choose the best
place for them self and their family.

## Calibrated bands — overall **4.0**

- **TR 4** — Responds to the prompt only minimally: there is no clear position (it

  are a thin list of generic pros and cons with no real support (TR-POSITION,
  TR-UNDERDEV). The "give your own opinion" part is not genuinely fulfilled.
- **CC 4** — There are paragraph breaks, but information is not arranged into any
  progressing argument; cohesion is basic and at times faulty, and there is no
  build toward the conclusion (CC-NOPROGRESSION).
- **LR 4** — Only basic vocabulary, repeated ("good", "bad thing", "many"), with

- **GRA 4** — Errors predominate and the range is very limited: missing articles
  and gerunds ("live in big city", "for find work"), agreement failures ("the

  punctuation. Subordination is rarely controlled (GRA-AGREEMENT, GRA-ARTICLE,
  GRA-PUNCT).
