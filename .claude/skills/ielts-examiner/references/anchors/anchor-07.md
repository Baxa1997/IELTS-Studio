## Prompt

Some people think that students should be allowed to use mobile phones in school.
To what extent do you agree or disagree?

## Essay (≈ 250 words)

Nowadays the mobile phone is very popular and many student bring it to the school.
Some people think this is good but other people think this is bad. In my opinion I
think it is good but also have some problem.

First of all, the mobile phone can help the student for study. For example when the
teacher give a difficult question, the student can search the answer in the internet
and they learn faster. Also the student can call to their parent if there is
emergency, so the parent don't worry. This is very important for the safety.

On the other hand, some student use the phone for play game and chat in the class.
This is very bad because they don't listen the teacher and they get low mark. Also
too much phone is not good for the eyes and the brain. Many student become addicted
and they can't stop.

In my opinion, I think the school should allow the phone but with some rule. For
example, the student can use the phone only in the break time, not in the class. If
everybody follow this rule, the phone will be helpful and not make problem.

In conclusion, the mobile phone has advantage and disadvantage. But if we use it in
correct way, I think it is more good than bad for the student in the school.

- **CC 5** — There is some organisation — paragraphs and a visible sequence
