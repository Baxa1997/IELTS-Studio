## Prompt

A friend has agreed to look after your house while you are away on holiday. Write a
letter to your friend. In your letter:
- give contact details for while you are away
- give instructions about how to care for your pet
- describe any other household tasks

Write at least 150 words. (Informal — the reader is a close friend.)

## Letter (≈ 190 words)

Hi Sam,

You're an absolute lifesaver for agreeing to keep an eye on the place while we're in
Japan — I really can't thank you enough.

I'll have my mobile on me the whole time, but since the signal can be patchy out there,
the surest way to reach me is by email, which I'll be checking every evening. If
anything truly urgent crops up, my sister Anna lives just around the corner and is
happy to help.

The main thing is Biscuit, our rather spoiled cat. He needs feeding twice a day —
there's a stack of food in the cupboard under the sink — and he'll sulk dramatically if
he misses his evening treat, so do indulge him. A quick brush every few days keeps him
content.

Other than that, it would be brilliant if you could water the plants on the balcony a
couple of times a week and put the bins out on Thursday morning. And please help
yourself to anything in the fridge!

Thanks again — I owe you one.

See you soon,
Jess
