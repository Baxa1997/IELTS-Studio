# Anchor — Task 2, Band 6.0 (original essay)

**Prompt:** Some people believe university students should pay the full cost of their studies, while others think higher education should be free for everyone. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

**Essay (as written by a band-6.0 candidate):**

> Nowadays, the cost of university is a big topic that many people discuss about. Some people think students must pay all the money for their study, but other people think education should be free. In this essay I will discuss both sides.
>
> On the one hand, some people believe students should pay full cost. Moreover, the government has not enough money for everything, so it is better if students pay. Moreover, when students pay their own money, they will study harder because they don't want to waste it. This is a good point.
>
> On the other hand, many people think education must be free for everyone. Moreover, education is a right and not only rich people should go to university. If education is free, poor students also can study and get good job. This helps the country because there are more educated people.
>
> In my opinion, I think education should be free or at least cheap. Because every person has the right to study and it is not fair if only rich people can go to university. So the government should help students with the cost.

---

## Grading

**Overall band: 6.0** (TR 6, CC 6, LR 6, GRA 6)

### TR — 6
Both views are addressed and an opinion is given, so the task is covered. But ideas are **underdeveloped** — each point is stated in one or two sentences with no real example (TR-03), and several sentences are filler ("This is a good point"). The opinion is present but thin.
*Evidence: TR-03 (every body point under-supported), no concrete examples anywhere.*

### CC — 6
There is a clear overall structure (intro / both sides / opinion), which keeps it at 6. But cohesion is **mechanical and faulty**: "Moreover" opens four sentences in a row, including where it should signal a first point, not an addition (CC-01, CC-02). Paragraphs hold together but the linking is the classic band-6 ceiling.
*Evidence: CC-01 ("Moreover" ×4), CC-02 (Moreover used to introduce, not add).*

### LR — 6
Range is adequate for the task and meaning is always clear, but vocabulary is basic and repetitive ("study", "money", "people" repeated; LR-05), with a collocation slip ("discuss about", LR-03) and "get good job" (article, also signals limited range).
*Evidence: LR-03 ("discuss about"), LR-05 (repetition of basic words).*

### GRA — 6
A mix of simple and a few complex sentences, but errors recur in the more complex attempts and articles are dropped ("get good job", "has not enough money"). Errors don't block meaning, which is exactly band 6.
*Evidence: GRA-01 (missing articles), GRA-06 (mostly simple structures).*

### Score-blocker
**Task Response (development).** The structure and control are already at 6; what caps the whole essay is that no idea is developed with explanation + example. Fixing TR-03 across the two body paragraphs is the fastest route to 6.5.

**Band with fixes:** 6.5 — develop each body point with a "because…" and one concrete example, and replace the repeated "Moreover" with varied/again natural linking.
